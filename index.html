ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE?

ECOMAZONS is a group of Women IT developers with diverse backgrounds and a vision to corroborate existing efforts; governmental and non-governmental, in riding our environments of plastic pollution whilst helping children from undesrved communities get tech savvy.

VISION

To create a brand that will make significant impact in communities as regards to the management of PET bottle waste, leading to a cleaner environment.

MISSION

To sensitize communities especially the rural areas on the dangers of indiscriminate dumping of PET bottles, introduce incentives for proper disposals and encourage the adoption of eco-friendly packaging.
